A pair of curved 3D-printed cradles that hold a laptop vertically on a desk, freeing up surface area while keeping the machine easy to grab. The cradles flare outwards at the base for stability and carry a subtle "BANJOS" wordmark embossed on the front.

Released on MakerWorld on 2023-12-27. Originally printed in a black-with-orange-stripe filament that matches the silkscreen accents.
