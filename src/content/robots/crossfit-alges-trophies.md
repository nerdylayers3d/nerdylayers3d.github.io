A set of 3D-printed kettlebell trophies commissioned for the CrossFit Algés 1st Anniversary "adversário" competition (a 2024 event commemorating the gym's opening, the original 2022 edition of which is referenced in the IG caption). Each trophy is a stylized kettlebell sitting on a flared base — the kettlebell carries the "CROSSFIT ALGES 1º ANIVERSÁRIO" shield, and the base is engraved with the placement and category (e.g. "2º LUGAR FEMININOS"). Printed in a matte dark filament for a cast-metal look.
