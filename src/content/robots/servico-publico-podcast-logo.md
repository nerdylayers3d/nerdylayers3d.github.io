A large 3D-printed signage piece spelling out **"SERVIÇO PÚBLICO"** in chunky two-layer letters (white face on a black base) for the new studio of the @1canalservicopublico podcast — plus a matching batch of miniature keychain versions handed out as gifts.

The lettering uses a heavy slab face with sharp counters, mounted directly on the studio wall. Posted on Instagram on 2024-11-04. Caption: "Logótipo para o novo estúdio do podcast @1canalservicopublico e uns miminhos em formato de porta-chaves."
