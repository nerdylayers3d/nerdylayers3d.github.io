![[avent-baby-monitor-mount-cover.png]]

A clip-on mount that lets the Philips Avent SCD630 baby monitor camera hook onto the rail of a cot, putting the lens directly over the sleeping baby without needing wall-mount hardware or a separate tripod. The mount cradles the camera's circular base and ends in a hook that wraps over the rail.

Released on MakerWorld on 2023-12-27.
