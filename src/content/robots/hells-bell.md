A large 3D-printed homage to AC/DC's iconic "Hell's Bell" stage prop — the cast-bronze foundry bell that drops from the rigging during *Hells Bells*. The model reproduces the canonical engravings: "TAYLOR LOUGHBOROUGH" around the crown (the original UK bell foundry), the AC/DC lightning-bolt logo on the body, and "HELL'S BELL" wrapping the lower band. Printed in dark grey filament for the patinated look.

Made as a gift for a friend (@hugo_s_ramos) who used to work at @barbearia44 — the IG caption describes it as a tribute to "his most rocker phase". Posted on Instagram on 2024-11-02.
