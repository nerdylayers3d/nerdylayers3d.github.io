\- High discharge rate and constant discharge curve make your RC vehicle run powerfully.  
  
Specifications:  
Brand: Zeee  
Number of cells: 3S.  
Voltage (V): 11.1V  
Capacity (mAh): 2200mAh  
Discharge Rate (C): 50°C

Discharge plug type: XT60 plug  
Battery Dimensions (±2mm): 75 x 34 x 26.5mm (L x W x H)  
Weight: about 137g  
  
Compatible RC Models:  
Zeee 3S 11.1V 50C 2200mAh rc lipo battery compatible with 1/10 rc car, rc truck, remote control vehicles, rc helicopter, airplane, FPV (only if the voltage, dimension and plug match, then it will fit)  
  
Attention:  
1\. Lipo batteries are potentially dangerous. Improper use can cause fire or explosion.  
2\. Use the professional Lipo battery charger.  
3\. Never overcharge or overdischarge the lipo battery.  
4\. Stop charging immediately when the cell voltage has been charged to 4.2V (the normal cell voltage is between 3.2V~4.2V).  
5\. If the new battery does not maintain a full charge or lose charge very quickly in a short time, please use the balance charger to charge and recycle multiple times first.
