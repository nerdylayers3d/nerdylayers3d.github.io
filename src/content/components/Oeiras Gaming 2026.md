# Event Prep: Oeiras Gaming 2026 - Lucille

This document tracks the preparation for **Lucille**'s appearance at the Oeiras Gaming 2026 event.

## 🛠 Technical Checklist

### Weapon System (The Redesigned Drum)
- [ ] **Check belt tension (GT2 200-2M-10); ensure no skipping under load.**
- [ ] **Inspect central pulley teeth for wear or debris.**
- [ ] **Tighten the 4x M5 screws on the aluminum sleeves (Check both asymmetric pairs: 2x close, 2x wide). Apply Blue Loctite.**
- [ ] Inspect the weapon shaft bearings for smooth rotation and debris.
- [ ] Balance the drum to minimize vibration at 25,000 RPM.
- [ ] Test weapon spin-up and active braking (ESC check).

### Drive System
- [ ] Verify 400RPM geared motors are secured in their mounts.
- [ ] **Check the 6x countersunk screws on the front 2mm steel wedge for loosening.**
- [ ] Check rubber wheel traction; clean with isopropyl alcohol if needed.
- [ ] Ensure drive chains/gears are lubricated and tensioned.

### Electronics & Power
- [ ] **Inspect the top-mounted XT30 safety link for heat damage or loose solder joints.**
- [ ] **Verify the black top-plate is securely fastened to the TPU chassis.**
- [ ] Charge all 3S 850mAh LiPo batteries to storage voltage, then cycle.
- [ ] Verify Failsafe: Robot must stop immediately on transmitter power-off.
- [ ] Check antenna mounting for the ER6 receiver (ensure it's away from carbon/metal).
- [ ] Secure all internal wiring with zip-ties to prevent "rat's nest" interference.

## 📦 Pit Kit & Spares
- [ ] **Charge all power tools (Drill, Dremel, etc.).**
- [ ] **Print extra TPU wheel hubs.**
- [ ] Spare 3D printed TPU chassis (V1.1).
- [ ] Spare PETG drum assembly (fully balanced).
- [ ] 2x Spare N20/25GA drive motors.
- [ ] LiPo Charging Station + Fire Bag.
- [ ] Tool Kit: Hex keys, soldering iron, spare M5 screws, and superglue (for emergency TPU repairs).

## 📋 Logistics & Control
- [ ] **Test inverted driving performance.**
- [ ] **Create inverted driving remote profile (channel mixing/reversing).**
- [ ] Print and bring technical specs sheet for tech inspection.
- [ ] Verify weight: Ensure final weight is ≤ 1500g (Portuguese Beetleweight limit).
- [ ] Pack "Inverted Driving" practice kit.

## 🎥 Media Strategy
- [ ] Charge GoPro/Camera batteries.
- [ ] Clear SD cards for "Pit Diary" and match footage.
- [ ] Plan "The Reveal" shot for Instagram/Facebook.
