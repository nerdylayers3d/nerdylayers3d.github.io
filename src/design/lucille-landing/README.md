## 🤖 Detailed Information Context (For Stitch AI)

### Robot Identity
- **Name:** LUCILLE
- **Class:** Beetleweight (1.5 kg / 3.3 lbs)
- **Concept:** Inspired by "Lucille" from *The Walking Dead* (Negan's barbed-wire bat). The design philosophy is "Brute Force" kinetic energy paired with a "Glass Cannon" structure that is built to survive through elastic deformation (TPU).

### Technical Specifications
- **Weapon System:** 310g Split Metal-Sleeved Drum.
    - **Sleeves:** 2x 1.5mm machined aluminum.
    - **Hardware:** 4x M5 Screws in asymmetric pairs (2 close, 2 wide) for max bite.
    - **Drive:** Central GT2 200-2M-10 belt drive (reduces slippage).
    - **Motor:** Brushless RS2205 2300kv (runs at ~25,000 RPM on 3S).
- **Drive System:** 
    - **Motors:** 2x 25GA-370 Geared DC Motors (400 RPM).
    - **Wheels:** Lego 56x28 ZR Tires (provides balance of grip and impact resistance).
- **Construction:**
    - **Chassis:** 10mm-30mm 3D-printed TPU (Red).
    - **Top Plate:** Black rigid composite.
    - **Wedge:** 45-degree 2mm Steel feeder wedge with 6x countersunk fasteners.
- **Electronics:** RadioMaster ER6 (ExpressLRS 2.4GHz), 3S 850mAh 70C LiPo.

### Mission & Strategy
Lucille is a "Distractor and Destroyer." The 2mm steel wedge slides under opponents to feed them into the spinning drum. The TPU chassis acts as a giant shock absorber, allowing the robot to bounce back from hits that would shatter rigid plastics.
